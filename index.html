// How I work

MY PROCESS

A clear, repeatable process that delivers consistent results on every project.

01

Discovery

Understanding your business goals, existing data infrastructure, key stakeholders, and the specific decisions you need to make better. This stage defines the scope and prevents expensive surprises later in the project.

02

Data Audit

Assessing data quality, completeness, and structure across all relevant sources. Identifying gaps, inconsistencies, and quick-win opportunities. Building a data dictionary and establishing cleaning protocols before any analysis begins.

03

Analysis

Running the actual analysis, modelling, or campaign build using the appropriate tools. Regular check-ins keep you informed of progress and allow course corrections early. All code and methodology is documented throughout this phase.

04

Delivery

Presenting findings in clear, decision-ready formats — dashboards, reports, slide decks, or live walkthroughs. Every deliverable includes an executive summary, key findings, and specific recommended actions ranked by potential impact.

05

Support

Post-delivery support to answer questions, refine outputs based on feedback, and train your team to maintain dashboards independently. Optional monthly retainer packages ensure continuous improvement and keep your analytics current as your business evolves.
