PART 9: TROUBLESHOOTING
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

PROBLEM: Not receiving emails
SOLUTION:
1. Check spam/junk folder
2. Make sure email is verified in Formspree
3. Check Formspree dashboard for submissions
4. Verify recipient email in settings

PROBLEM: Form won't submit
SOLUTION:
1. Check all fields are filled (name, email, subject, message)
2. Check email format is valid
3. Wait 30 seconds and try again
4. Clear browser cache and try again

PROBLEM: Auto-reply not working
SOLUTION:
1. Log in to Formspree
2. Check auto-reply is turned ON
3. Check reply subject is set
4. Check reply body has content
5. Click "Save" and try again

PROBLEM: Too many spam submissions
SOLUTION:
1. Enable reCAPTCHA (in Settings)
2. Monitor submissions in Formspree
3. Mark spam as spam in dashboard
4. Consider paid plan for better filtering
