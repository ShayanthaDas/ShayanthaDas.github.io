Why Data Analytics is Important for Business Growth

Data analytics helps businesses understand what is working and what is not. Companies use data to reduce risk, improve marketing strategies, and increase revenue.

In modern industries, data-driven decision making is more important than intuition.

How Marketing Analytics Improves ROI

Marketing analytics allows businesses to track campaign performance, customer engagement, and return on investment.

This helps companies stop wasting budget on low-performing ads and focus on high-performing channels.
