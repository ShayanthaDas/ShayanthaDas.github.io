About Me

Data Analyst & BI Specialist

Turning raw data into clear, actionable business insights.

I am Shayantha Das, a Data Analyst and Business Intelligence Specialist from Chattogram, Bangladesh. With an MBA in Marketing from Comilla University and three years of hands-on experience building 15+ end-to-end analytics projects, I've learned that great data analysis is about asking the right questions first, then answering them with precision.

My technical expertise spans the complete analytics pipeline: data extraction and cleaning with SQL and Python, exploratory data analysis with Pandas and NumPy, statistical modeling and machine learning with Scikit-Learn, and final communication through interactive Power BI and Tableau dashboards designed for non-technical stakeholders.

What sets me apart is my hybrid background in marketing analytics and data science. I understand what business questions matter, how to measure success, and how to translate technical findings into strategic recommendations. Every project I take on aims to reduce costs, grow revenue, or accelerate decision-making.

I'm currently open to Data Analyst, BI Analyst, MIS Executive, and Marketing Analyst roles both locally in Bangladesh and for international remote positions. I respond within 24 hours and bring both depth and clarity to every engagement.
